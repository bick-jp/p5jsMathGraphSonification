Description of the graph

The graph is y equals two. The graph has x- and y-axes and the scale for both axes is in units of one, labeled from minus five to plus five. The shape of the graph is a straight horizontal line. The graph begins at the point of x equals minus five and y equals plus two. As it moves to the right, the graph continues to be constantly two until it reaches the point of x equals plus five and y equals plus two.

Sonification

Click a play button to start sonification.

Description of sonification

You will hear the constant pitch from beginning to end.
